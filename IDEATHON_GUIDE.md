## ğŸ¯ Judges' Questions - Prepared Answers

**Q: How will you get authorities to use this?**
A: Start with MoU with progressive wards, demonstrate value through pilot results, show improved citizen satisfaction metrics.

**Q: How do you prevent spam/fake reports?**
A: Multi-layer verification: community voting, photo evidence, location validation, volunteer verification, reputation system.

**Q: What about digital divide?**
A: SMS gateway, offline mode, volunteer-assisted reporting, IVR system, community kiosks.

**Q: Revenue model?**
A: Government contracts, NGO partnerships, premium analytics for municipalities, grant funding, corporate CSR.

**Q: Existing competition?**
A: We focus on transparency + youth engagement + gamification. Others are just reporting tools.
